Current Healthcare Barriers

  • Limited Specialists: Scarcity of trained cytopathologists in sub-Saharan Africa
  • Geographic Inequality: Expensive diagnostic equipment concentrated in urban centers
  • Late-Stage Detection: Most cases discovered when treatment options are severely limited
  • Resource Constraints: Rural clinics lack access to expert-level screening capabilities

Our Solution: Democratize expert-level cervical cancer screening by making it accessible in remote clinics and resource-constrained settings, combining AI-powered image analysis with demographic risk factors for comprehensive early detection.
